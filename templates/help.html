How to Play?

We will randomly select a League of Legends ranked match. You may reveal one clue by clicking on the button beside the champion you choose. Using all the information you have, guess which team is winning!

Specification

  • All matches are from the KR region Master-to-Challenger League.
  • You will get 1 point if you guess it right, and your score resets to zero when you're wrong.
  • Login to store your own record!
